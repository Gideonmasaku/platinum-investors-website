FAQs

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Create Account" button on the homepage, fill in your details, and follow the instructions.

How can I apply for a loan?

Go to the "Loan Services" page and click on "Apply for Loan." Fill in the necessary details to submit your application.

How is my interest calculated?

Interest is calculated annually based on your total contributions and the current rate offered by Platinum Investors.
